Identification and Authentication  


Identification and Authentication is the process of verifying that an identity is bound to the entity that makes an asseration or claim of identity.The Identification and Authentication process assumes that there was an initial Validation of the identity, commanly called identity proffing.The only Requirements for the identifier is that it must be unique within its security domain.
Authenticators are commenly based on atleast one of the following four factors.

  1. Something you know such as a password or a personal identificationNumber (PIN). This assumes that only the owner of the account knows the password or PIN needed to access the account.
  2. Something you have such as smartcard or security token.This assumes that only the owner of account has the neccessory smartcards or token needed to unlock the account.
  3. Something you are such as fingerprint, voice, ratina or iris charecterstics.
  4. Where you are, for example inside or outside a company firewall, or proximity of Client location to a personal GPS device.
